Created with care and a little whimsy on Main Street in Lexington, Virginia, The Georges currently offers five elegantly comfortable rooms in our historic Alexander-Withrow House, graced with every modern amenity.

Haywood's is named after a man who liked to eat well; who loved Dixieland jazz and swing music; and who never met a stranger, and never saw a piano bar, he didn't like. Our restaurant, on the main floor of the Alexander-Withrow House, is a place Haywood would've loved. In his honor, we serve cocktails and small plates—all lovingly crafted with the finest fresh, local ingredients.
